What?

This site is a place for students to find other students who are persuing similar goals.

We've been in those overcrowded lectures, where every face is anonymous. We've faced those exams, where it would be great to have peers but you don't know who to call.

This site is for people who want to reach out and learn together. Try a studysesh.
